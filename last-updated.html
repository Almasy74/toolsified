Currency Data – Last Updated

Toolsified's currency converter uses real-time exchange rates from the free and open-source API: exchangerate.host.

The exchange rates are typically updated once per day based on data from the European Central Bank and other sources.

On each conversion, the converter fetches the latest available rate from the API and shows the date of the last update under the result.

For most use cases, this is accurate enough. However, if you need up-to-the-minute or historical rates, please consult a financial data provider.
