Currency Converter

What this tool does

This currency converter gives you a live mid-market reference rate for any supported pair. Enter an amount, pick source and target currencies, and get an instant result. It is useful for travel budgeting, freelance invoicing, procurement screening, and quick FX checks before settlement.

Reference rate vs. execution rate: the rate shown here is a market midpoint. When you exchange money through a bank, card, or transfer service, the provider adds spread (typically 0.3%–3%) and possibly fixed fees. This converter helps you estimate the baseline; compare execution options separately.

How the conversion works

The formula: converted amount = entered amount × latest rate. Rates are fetched from an external API feed via a serverless endpoint. Multiplication runs client-side. No account or login required.

Limitations: this is a reference converter. Real settlements differ due to provider spread, card network rates, transfer fees, and timing. For formal accounting, use approved treasury rates. Read full methodology.

Common use cases

FAQ

How accurate is this live currency converter?

It uses mid-market reference rates from an external API. Actual bank or card rates include spread and fees that are not reflected here.

Why does my bank show a different amount?

Banks apply their own spread, service fees, and settlement timing. This tool shows a market reference, not institution-specific pricing.

Can I use these rates for accounting?

For planning only. Accounting close requires approved policy rates with documented timestamps from your organization.

Is login required?

No. All conversions run without an account.
