How it works

The conversion logic is straightforward: converted amount = input amount x exchange rate. When you press Convert, the page requests a current market rate through /.netlify/functions/convert, then multiplies the returned rate by your amount in the browser. No account is required, and the arithmetic runs client-side after the rate is returned.

Data source and timing: this tool depends on an external exchange-rate feed accessed via the serverless endpoint. The displayed "last updated" timestamp reflects the provider response for the queried pair.

Limitations: this is a reference converter, not a settlement engine. Real transaction totals can differ because of provider markups, card network rates, local taxes, transfer fees, weekends, and delayed booking windows. For accounting, treasury, and compliance use, confirm with your payment provider or bank statement.
