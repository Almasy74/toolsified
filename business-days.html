Business Days Calculator

Intro

This calculator counts business days between two dates and can exclude weekends and selected national holiday calendars. It is useful for contract deadlines, payroll cycles, service-level commitments, procurement lead times, and internal project planning.

How it works

The logic iterates date by date from start to end (inclusive), tracks total days, flags weekend dates, and checks holiday matches from built-in country rules. Business-day output is the remaining set after exclusions.

Limitations: public holiday rules are simplified and may not include all regional observances, one-off decrees, or company-specific non-working days.

Practical use scenarios

  • Estimate payment due dates for net terms.
  • Calculate SLA windows excluding weekends and holidays.
  • Plan onboarding timelines across countries.
  • Forecast staffing workload around holiday periods.

FAQ

Are start and end dates included?

Yes, the range is inclusive.

Can I exclude weekends?

Yes, enable the weekend exclusion checkbox.

Are holiday calendars complete?

They are practical defaults and should be validated for legal or payroll-critical processes.

Does the tool support custom company holidays?

Not currently; use output as a baseline and apply internal calendar adjustments.
