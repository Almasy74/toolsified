Inflation Calculator

Intro

This calculator estimates how purchasing power changes over time between two years. It is useful for salary comparisons, long-term budgeting, savings goals, and historical price context.

How it works

The tool compounds annual inflation rates year by year from selected start year to end year. Formula: adjusted value = amount x product(1 + annual rate).

Limitations: this is an educational estimate based on simplified annual CPI-like rates and does not replace official national statistics for legal or contractual use.

Practical use scenarios

  • Compare historical salary offers in today's purchasing power.
  • Estimate how much savings targets should be adjusted over time.
  • Add inflation context to project or grant budgeting.
  • Explain long-term cost growth in presentations.
