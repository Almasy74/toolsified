About Toolsified

Toolsified is an independent web tools publisher based in Norway. The site is operated as a focused utility platform for practical calculations related to time zones, currencies, crypto analysis, business-day planning, and inflation estimation.

Who is behind the site

Toolsified is managed by an independent operator and small editorial-maintenance workflow. The platform is not a bank, broker, tax authority, law firm, or government entity. We build and maintain utility pages, update rate connections, review technical quality, and publish methodology notes so users can understand what each calculator does and does not do.

Purpose of the website

The purpose is to provide clear, fast, and free browser-based tools with enough explanatory context to support real decisions. We emphasize practical value: what the tool is for, how it calculates output, where source dependencies exist, and what limitations apply. This helps users avoid over-trusting a calculator in high-stakes contexts.

How data is handled

Most calculation steps run locally in the browser. Some tools request external reference data through serverless endpoints (for example live exchange rates or historical market rates). We do not require user accounts to use the public tools. Contact submissions occur only when users email us directly.

Editorial and quality approach

  • Each tool page includes methodology, use-case context, and FAQ coverage.
  • We maintain links between related tools for better navigation and topic depth.
  • We publish core policy pages: About, Contact, Privacy, and Terms.
  • We continuously revise content quality and technical reliability.
